ABOUT US

Mission

To empower gamers by providing accessible, affordable, and efficient game credits and premium services tailored to enhance their gaming journey. At CredZ, we aim to build trust and long-term relationships with our customers through exceptional service, transparency, and a deep understanding of the gaming community’s needs. By prioritizing innovation and customer satisfaction, we strive to make every gamer’s experience smoother, more enjoyable, and more rewarding.

Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in the gaming community by offering unmatched value, convenience, and reliability in game credits and piloting services. We envision a future where every gamer, regardless of their background or resources, can access the tools they need to excel and find joy in their gaming adventures. Through continuous improvement and customer-focused innovation, CredZ aspires to shape the gaming marketplace with integrity and excellence.

Company History

CredZ began its journey under the name Yua’s Shop, founded by a passionate gamer determined to provide affordable game credits to friends and fellow players. What started as a small, personal venture quickly grew into a recognized brand within the gaming community. Yua’s Shop earned trust through its dedication to quality and customer care.

Over time, the shop expanded its ownership to include three more partners: Knothey, Vincent, and Dayvid. With the collective effort of these four owners, Yua’s Shop was rebranded to CredZ, offering discounted game credits for popular games like Mobile Legends, Valorant, COD Mobile, and Roblox. This transformation marked a significant milestone, as the brand became synonymous with reliability and affordability. Despite its growth, CredZ remains committed to its humble beginnings, continuing to serve gamers with the same passion and care that inspired its creation.
